## Doctor Doom
*Medium humanoid (human), lawful evil*

___
**Armor Class** 23 (Armor of Doom)
**Hit Points** 367 (35d8 + 210)
**Speed** 30 ft., fly 60 ft. (hover)
___

**Saving Throws** Con +13, Int +16, Wis +12, Cha +14
**Skills** Arcana +23, History +16, Insight +12, Intimidation +14, Investigation +16, Perception +12
**Damage Resistances** cold, fire, lightning, thunder; bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing from nonmagical attacks

**Condition Immunities** poisoned
**Senses** truesight 120 ft., passive Perception 22
**Languages** all languages
**Challenge** 25 (75,000 XP) **Proficiency Bonus** +7
___

***Armor of Doom.*** Doom's armor grants him immunity to disease and he has no need to eat, drink, breathe, or sleep. The armor cannot be removed against his will and repairs itself, regaining 10 hit points at the start of each of Doom's turns if it has at least 1 hit point.

***Indomitable Will.*** Doom is immune to any effect that would sense his emotions, read his thoughts, or magically determine if he is lying. Additionally, if he is subjected to *dominate person*, *dominate monster*, or similar effects, he automatically succeeds on the saving throw.

***Legendary Resistance (3/Day).*** If Doom fails a saving throw, he can choose to succeed instead.

***Magic Resistance.*** Doom has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.

***Ruler of Latveria.*** Doom has advantage on Charisma checks made to interact with creatures that recognize his authority or political power. Additionally, creatures have disadvantage on Insight checks made against him.

***Supreme Sorcerer.*** Doom's spellcasting ability is Intelligence (spell save DC 24, +16 to hit with spell attacks). Doom can cast *shield* at will (included in his AC calculations where applicable) without expending a spell slot. He has the following spells prepared:

Cantrips (at will): *fire bolt, mage hand, minor illusion, prestidigitation, shocking grasp*
1st level (4 slots): *detect magic, magic missile*
2nd level (3 slots): *hold person, mirror image, misty step*
3rd level (3 slots): *counterspell, dispel magic, fireball, lightning bolt*
4th level (3 slots): *banishment, dimension door, fire shield*
5th level (3 slots): *hold monster, telekinesis, wall of force*
6th level (2 slots): *chain lightning, disintegrate, globe of invulnerability*
7th level (2 slots): *forcecage, plane shift, teleport*
8th level (1 slot): *dominate monster, power word stun*
9th level (1 slot): *power word kill, time stop*

### Actions

***Multiattack.*** Doom makes three attacks using Gauntlet Strike, Plasma Bolt, or a combination of the two. He can replace one attack with a casting of a cantrip.

***Gauntlet Strike.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +12 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 14 (2d8 + 5) bludgeoning damage plus 18 (4d8) lightning damage. The target must succeed on a DC 21 Constitution saving throw or be stunned until the end of its next turn. A creature can be stunned by this attack only once per turn.

***Plasma Bolt.*** *Ranged Spell Attack:* +16 to hit, range 150 ft., one target. *Hit:* 27 (4d8 + 9) force damage plus 13 (3d8) fire damage.

Each creature in that area must make a DC 24 Dexterity saving throw, taking 72 (16d8) force damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. A creature reduced to 0 hit points by this damage is disintegrated, along with any nonmagical objects it is wearing or carrying.

***Summon Doombots (1/Day).*** Doom summons 2 **Doombots** (use **Helmed Horror** statistics with the following changes: Int 14, speaks all languages, and can cast *fire bolt* at will). The Doombots appear in unoccupied spaces within 60 feet of Doom, act on their own initiative, obey his commands, and vanish after 1 hour or when destroyed.

### Bonus Actions

***Force Field.*** Doom activates or deactivates his personal force field. While active, he has resistance to all damage, but his speed is halved and he cannot fly.

***Tactical Teleport.*** Doom teleports up to 30 feet to an unoccupied space he can see.

### Reactions

***Deflection Matrix.*** When Doom is hit by a ranged attack or targeted by *magic missile*, he can use his reaction to deflect it. He takes no damage from *magic missile* and reduces the damage from a ranged attack by 35 (10d6). If this reduces the damage to 0, Doom can redirect the attack at a creature within 60 feet, using the original attack roll against the new target.

***Superior Counterspell (3/Day).*** When a creature Doom can see casts a spell of 3rd level or lower, he can use his reaction to automatically counter the spell without expending a spell slot.

### Legendary Actions

Doom can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action option can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. Doom regains spent legendary actions at the start of his turn.

**Detect.** Doom makes a Wisdom (Perception) check or uses *detect magic*.

**Plasma Bolt (Costs 2 Actions).** Doom makes one Plasma Bolt attack.

**Technomantic Assault (Costs 2 Actions).** Doom causes one technological device or metal object he can see within 60 feet to malfunction. If a creature is wearing or holding the object, it must succeed on a DC 24 Dexterity saving throw or take 22 (4d10) lightning damage and drop the object. Constructs and creatures in heavy armor have disadvantage on this save.

**Cast a Spell (Costs 3 Actions).** Doom casts a spell of 5th level or lower from his list of prepared spells, expending a spell slot as normal.

___

### Lair Actions

When fighting inside Castle Doom or another location he has prepared, Doom can take lair actions. On initiative count 20 (losing initiative ties), Doom can take one of the following lair actions; he can't take the same lair action two rounds in a row:

- **Automated Defenses.** Concealed turrets target up to three creatures Doom can see. Each target must succeed on a DC 21 Dexterity saving throw or take 21 (6d6) force damage.

- **Lockdown Protocol.** All doors within the lair magically seal until initiative count 20 of the next round. A sealed door can be forced open with a successful DC 25 Strength check or destroyed (AC 18, 50 HP, immunity to poison and psychic damage).

- **Neural Scrambler.** Each creature of Doom's choice within 60 feet of a technological device must succeed on a DC 21 Intelligence saving throw or be stunned until initiative count 20 of the next round.

- **Teleportation Grid.** Doom and up to five willing creatures he can see within 120 feet each teleport to an unoccupied space of Doom's choice within the lair.

> ### Doombot Deception
> *At the DM's discretion, any encounter with Doctor Doom may actually be a Doombot duplicate in disguise. When a "Doom" is reduced to 0 hit points, there is a chance (determined by the DM) that it is revealed to be a Doombot, exploding for 28 (8d6) fire damage to all creatures within 15 feet (DC 18 Dexterity save for half). The real Doom observes from a safe location, gaining intelligence on his foes.*
