## DOCTOR DOOM
*Medium humanoid (human), lawful evil*

___

**Armor Class** 23 (Armor of Doom)

**Hit Points** 367 (35d8 + 210)

**Speed** 30 ft., fly 60 ft. (hover)

**Saving Throws** Con +13, Int +16, Wis +12, Cha +14

**Skills** Arcana +16, History +16, Insight +12, Intimidation +14, Perception +12

**Damage Resistances** cold, fire, lightning, thunder; bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing from nonmagical attacks

**Damage Immunities** poison

**Condition Immunities** charmed, frightened, poisoned

**Senses** truesight 120 ft., passive Perception 22

**Languages** Common, Abyssal, Celestial, Draconic, Infernal, Primordial

**Challenge** 24 (62,000 XP) &emsp;&emsp;&emsp; **Proficiency Bonus** +7

___

***Armor of Doom.*** Doom's armor is a masterwork fusion of science and sorcery. While wearing it, Doom doesn't need to breathe, eat, or sleep, and he can survive in the vacuum of space. The armor can't be removed against his will. If Doom would be moved against his will, he reduces the distance by up to 30 feet.

***Diplomatic Immunity.*** Doom is the sovereign ruler of Latveria. In settings that recognize his sovereignty, he technically cannot be arrested or legally detained, though this offers no mechanical protection against combat damage.

***Indomitable Will.*** Doom is immune to any effect that would sense his emotions, read his thoughts, or magically put him to sleep. Divination spells automatically fail if used to target or locate Doom unless he allows it.

***Legendary Resistance (3/Day).*** If Doom fails a saving throw, he can choose to succeed instead.

***Magic Resistance.*** Doom has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.

***Spellcasting.*** Doom is a 20th-level spellcaster. His spellcasting ability is Intelligence (spell save DC 24, +16 to hit with spell attacks). He has the following wizard spells prepared:

Cantrips (at will): *fire bolt*, *mage hand*, *minor illusion*, *prestidigitation*, *shocking grasp*
1st level (4 slots): *detect magic*, *magic missile*, *shield*
2nd level (3 slots): *hold person*, *misty step*, *scorching ray*
3rd level (3 slots): *counterspell*, *dispel magic*, *fireball*
4th level (3 slots): *banishment*, *dimension door*, *greater invisibility*
5th level (3 slots): *dominate person*, *hold monster*, *wall of force*
6th level (2 slots): *chain lightning*, *disintegrate*, *globe of invulnerability*
7th level (2 slots): *finger of death*, *forcecage*, *teleport*
8th level (1 slot): *dominate monster*, *maze*
9th level (1 slot): *power word kill*, *time stop*

***Supreme Arrogance.*** If a creature creates an illusion of Reed Richards or mocks Doom's intelligence (DM's discretion), Doom develops a singular focus on them. He has disadvantage on attack rolls against other targets until he deals damage to the offending creature.

### Actions

***Multiattack.*** Doom makes three attacks using Gauntlet Strike, Energy Blast, or a combination of both. He can replace one attack with casting a spell of 3rd level or lower.

***Gauntlet Strike.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +13 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 17 (2d10 + 6) bludgeoning damage plus 14 (4d6) lightning damage.

***Energy Blast.*** *Ranged Spell Attack:* +16 to hit, range 150 ft., one target. *Hit:* 32 (5d10 + 5) force damage.

### Bonus Actions

***Force Field.*** Doom recharges his deflective plating, gaining 20 temporary hit points.

***Tactical Teleport.*** Doom magically teleports up to 60 feet to an unoccupied space he can see.

### Reactions

***Power Absorption.*** When Doom succeeds on a saving throw against a spell of 6th level or lower, or when such a spell misses him, he absorbs the spell's energy. Is doing so, the caster takes force damage equal to 5 times the spell's level.

### Legendary Actions

Doom can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action option can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. Doom regains spent legendary actions at the start of his turn.

**Detect.** Doom makes a Wisdom (Perception) or Intelligence (Arcana) check.

**Energy Blast.** Doom makes one Energy Blast attack.

**Electroshock (Costs 2 Actions).** Each creature within 20 feet of Doom must succeed on a DC 24 Constitution saving throw or take 27 (6d8) lightning damage and be stunned until the end of that creature's next turn. On a success, a creature takes half damage and isn't stunned.

**Cast a Spell (Costs 3 Actions).** Doom casts a prepared spell of 3rd level or lower, expending a spell slot as normal.

## Lair Actions

On initiative count 20 (losing initiative ties), Doom can take one lair action to cause one of the following effects; Doom can't use the same effect two rounds in a row:

- **Automated Defenses.** Hidden weapons activate. Up to three creatures Doom can see must each make a DC 18 Dexterity saving throw, taking 28 (8d6) piercing damage on a failed save, or half as much on a success.

- **Lockdown.** Each creature of Doom's choice within the lair must succeed on a DC 18 Strength saving throw or be restrained by metal restraints until initiative count 20 of the next round. A restrained creature can use its action to attempt a DC 18 Strength (Athletics) check, freeing itself on a success.

- **Deploy Doombot.** A Doombot appears in an unoccupied space within 60 feet of Doom and acts on initiative count 10. The Doombot is a Medium construct with AC 16, 60 HP, and immunity to poison/psychic damage. It has one action: *Force Beam (+9 to hit, range 120 ft., 22 force damage).* It has the **Self-Sacrifice** reaction: If Doom takes damage while within 30 feet of a Doombot, the Doombot magically swaps places with Doom and takes the damage instead.

- **Power Surge.** Each creature of Doom's choice that is concentrating on a spell must succeed on a DC 18 Constitution saving throw or lose concentration. Doom regains one expended spell slot of 4th level or lower.

## Regional Effects

The region within 6 miles of Doom's primary lair is altered by his technology and magic:

- Doom is instantly aware when any creature speaks his name within this region.
- Doom can cast *scrying* at will (no components required) targeting any creature within 1 mile of his lair.
- Surveillance throughout the region makes it impossible to surprise Doom while he is in his lair, and he has advantage on initiative rolls there.

If Doom is destroyed, these effects end immediately, but the technological infrastructure remains functional for 1d10 days.
